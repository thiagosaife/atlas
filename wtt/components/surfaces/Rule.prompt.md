Ornamental divider: two ink lines meeting a diamond-and-ring compass motif (also available as `assets/textures/rule-compass.svg`).

```jsx
<h1 style={{ fontFamily: "var(--font-display)", letterSpacing: "var(--track-hero)" }}>Atlas of Long Gone Empires</h1>
<Rule />
```

Use under titles and to close sections. For plain separators inside panels use `border-bottom: 1px solid var(--ink-faint)` instead.
