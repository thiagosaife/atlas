The system's card: 135° paper gradient, brass border, warm floating shadow, square corners.

```jsx
<ParchmentCard style={{ width: 260 }}>…</ParchmentCard>
<ParchmentCard translucent>floats over the map at 0.94 alpha</ParchmentCard>
<ParchmentCard drawer style={{ width: 340, height: "100%" }}>side panel</ParchmentCard>
```

- `translucent` — for chrome floating over content (timeline bar, legend, search)
- `drawer` — right-hand side panel: 3px double brass left edge, leftward shadow
